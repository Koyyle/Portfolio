"Experiment facility" project
A survival horror game inspired by scp. The player has to avoid enemies and convince AI to help them escape. There were three endings.
Project Details / Background
Began development in 2018, and went into hiatus in 2019. The game was near completion, but developer burnout prevented further development to be made. The completed work has stopped working over the years, and is no longer available.
I've begun reimagining this game in the Unity engine, but don't have much to show at the moment besides concepts and ideas. This game is the precursor to Linear Rift, and I plan on completing it after Linear Rift is released.
